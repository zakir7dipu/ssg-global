Super Star Group HR Framework

Working at Super Star Group (SSG) would be a heartening and gratifying experience for sophisticated professional in SSG employees are always been treated more than their designation. Through strategic partnerships and alliance, the HR Department attracts, develops and retains a high performing, inclusive and diverse workforce, where we follow three pillars of the HR ecosystem:

Discover the right talent with frameworks
Develop them to become future-ready
Disrupt business outcomes by empowering them to become leaders of tomorrow

Then again, SSG fosters a safe, healthy, well-resourced and productive work environment for employees, their stakeholders in order to exploit individual potential, increase organizational capacity and ultimately appears in the business market as an employer of choice.

Our values serve as our foundation, informing every decision we make and guiding our operations. They are part of a shared vision that keeps us moving forward together.
HR EXCELLENCE AND INNOVATION

HR Excellence and innovation that results in effective outcomes and propels the organization forward while utilizing HR technologies and remaining a financial steward.

INVESTING IN OUR EMPLOYEES' DEVELOPMENT

Investing in the development of our employees and enhancing our succession management programs to support our "Best of the best vision.

TALENT HUNT AND RETENTION STRATEGIES

Strategies for attracting and retention of talent, including marketing and communication, to attract and retention of competent and diverse personnels to the organization. We're also working to improve the hr system, hold interactive learning events, offer a career preparation program, and help potential future leaders acquire business growth skill.

IMPROVING THE EMPLOYEE EXPERIENCE

improving the employee experience through a culture of employee engagement, wellness, diversity, and inclusion, which results in complete employee well-being, productivity, and retention
