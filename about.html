About Us

Super Star Group's (SSG) journey began with a fairly straightforward vision: to make world-class electrical products available to the people of Bangladesh at an inexpensive price that would not only be acceptable to everyone but would also propel the company to the top of its industry. After a more than two-decade journey, SSG has risen to the top of the list of people's preferred electrical solution providers.

The company began its journey in 1994 by creating its first endeavor, an incandescent light manufacturing in Narayanganj. Later on, the company expanded its product categories to include Electrical Accessories, CFL, LED lighting, Fan, Cables, Solar, Holdings, and Lifts, among others. SSG also established itself in the hygiene area with tissue products, with the phrase "Sharing is Caring" at its center. The company has won the coveted Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Industrial Award 2020, which was first introduced in Bangladesh. The award was granted by the Ministry of Industries in recognition of SSG's excellent contribution to the country's industrial development.
